Our mission is to make the performance of wind-assisted propulsion transparent and understandable through reliable and insightful data - for both our customers and our organization.

Our vision is to evolve into a data-driven technology company, enabling transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement across the maritime sector.

We operate a global network of computing units deployed on vessels worldwide. These computing units transmit data to our cloud infrastructure. This data then gets enriched with additional information from our external partners. This allows us to provide a combined view with various datasets, including:
- Operational data from our VentoFoil systems
- Engine room data
- AIS data
- Weather data
